What is bughouse

Bughouse is a chess variant that is played by four players divided into two teams of two players each. Allied players work together to checkmate someone from the other side or make them run out of time. Bughouse is amusing and exciting to play, mainly because of its special capturing rules and usually fast time controls. Also known as Siamese Chess, Swap Chess, and Exchange Chess, Bughouse is one of the most well-known modifications of chess in the world.
